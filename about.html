Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the purpose of this website?

This website aims to provide useful health tips and activities for seniors to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

2. How can I register for an account?

You can register by clicking on the "Log In / Register" button at the top-right corner and following the instructions.

3. Are the health tips on this website medically approved?

While our content is researched, we recommend consulting a medical professional before making any health-related decisions.

4. How can I contact support?

You can reach us via the "Contact Us" page where you will find our email and other contact details.

5. Is this website free to use?

Yes, all our content and features are free for everyone to use.
